Frequently Asked Questions

Question & Answers

How do I play?

Simply feel free to join any of our public servers in the game of your choosing. You're also welcome to join our public discord voice channels for each of those servers.

Is there any age restrictions?

In order to provide a fun and respectible experience for everyone, we require members to be 18+. This allows us to not be restricted by age limited content.

How do I join Fear and Terror?

If you meet all the requirements, feel free to submmit an application here. It will be reviewed by one of our staff members and you'll be invited for an interview.

Who is in Fear and Terror?

Fear and Terror consists of a lot of like minded adults that play games and have fun. While we take our gaming seriously, we try not to take ourselves too seriously.
